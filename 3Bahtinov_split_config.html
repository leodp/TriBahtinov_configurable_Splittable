Tri Bahtinov Mask, splittable, configurable

Tri-Bahtinov mask generator, based on Satakagi implementation. The mask can be split, to allow bigger masks to be 3D printed. Slits can be rotated of an offset angle. Programmed by Leonardo Della Pietra leodp@yahoo.com
